Aglio e Olio

Garlic and Oil

Aglio e Olio is a traditional italian dish. Literally meaning "garlic and oil," it is created by simmering finely-chopped garlic in olive oil until golden brown and soft, to which noodles are added. The dish is then topped with fresh parsley and red pepper flakes, giving a refreshing, rustic flavor with a bit of a kick!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley
  • 1 pound spaghetti noodles
  • 2 tbsp red pepper flakes

Directions

  1. Fill a pot with water, add salt, and set it to boil.
  2. Simmer olive oil at medium heat in a pan.
  3. Peel and finely chop garlic.
  4. Add noodles to boiling water.
  5. Add garlic to olive oil.
  6. Stir garlic regularly until it becomes golden-brown and soft.
  7. Add cooked noodles to pan.
  8. Pour 1/2 cup of pasta water into pan.
  9. Top with parsley and red pepper flakes.
  10. Stir until thorougly combined.
  11. Enjoy!
