BLT Sandwich

BLT: Bacon Lettuce Tomato

A bacon, lettuce, and tomato, or BLT, sandwich is a great way to start your day off with something healthy without having to sacrifice a delicious flavor. The crispy bacon, mixed with fresh lettuce, a juicy tomato, and light coating of mayo make for a delectible breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bacon
  • 2 leaves lettuce
  • 3 slice tomato
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 slices bread
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Fry bacon until crispy and evenly cooked.
  2. Toast bread to desired level.
  3. Add bacon to top of one piece of toast.
  4. Add lettuce on top of bacon.
  5. Add tomato slices on top of lettuce.
  6. Spread mayo on other piece of toast.
  7. Salt and pepper mayo.
  8. Put toast with mayo on top of tomato.
  9. Slice and enjoy!
